# Weather in Europe

<!-- Current weather overview for Europe -->

Europe is currently experiencing a mix of conditions typical for late July. Western Europe, including the UK and France, is seeing warm temperatures with occasional thunderstorms moving in from the Atlantic. Central Europe enjoys mostly sunny skies with temperatures ranging from 25 to 35 degrees Celsius, while the Iberian Peninsula remains hot and dry under a persistent high-pressure system. Northern Scandinavia has milder, overcast weather with some rainfall, and the Mediterranean coast continues to bask in clear skies and high humidity. Eastern Europe is transitioning through a brief cool spell following recent storm systems that swept through the region.
