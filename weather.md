# Weather in Europe

<!-- Placeholder: current weather overview for Europe -->

Europe is currently experiencing a mix of summer conditions typical for late July. Western regions such as the UK and France are seeing mild to warm temperatures with occasional Atlantic-driven cloud cover and light rain, while central Europe enjoys sunny spells with highs in the mid-to-upper 20s Celsius. Southern Europe, including Spain, Italy, and Greece, remains under a persistent heat dome bringing dry, sunny weather and temperatures frequently exceeding 35°C. Northern and Scandinavian countries enjoy long daylight hours with comfortable warmth, rarely exceeding 25°C. Overall, the continent presents a broad spectrum of summer weather, from temperate and breezy in the northwest to hot and dry in the south.
