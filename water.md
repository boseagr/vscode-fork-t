# Water

Europe is currently experiencing a mixed weather pattern, with Atlantic low-pressure systems bringing mild, wet conditions to western regions such as the British Isles, France, and the Iberian Peninsula, while central and eastern parts of the continent see more stable, high-pressure influenced weather with cooler temperatures and clearer skies. Northern Scandinavia remains cold with occasional snowfall at higher elevations, and the Mediterranean basin enjoys relatively warm and dry conditions typical of the season.
